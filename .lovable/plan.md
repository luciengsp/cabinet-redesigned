

## Cabinet PH - Redesigned Website

A modern, elegant redesign of the Cabinet Ponzini Hanser accounting firm website with the same content but a significantly improved user experience.

### Design Direction
- **Elegant & Corporate** aesthetic with refined typography and sophisticated layouts
- **Blue color palette** maintaining the professional feel of the original but modernized
- **Smooth animations** throughout for a premium, polished experience

---

### 1. Homepage (Accueil)
- **Hero section** with a welcoming headline, brief tagline about the firm's expertise since 1976, and a subtle animation
- **About the firm** section introducing Claude Ponzini & Catherine Hanser as partners
- **Real estate specializations** displayed as elegant cards showcasing their areas of expertise (Property companies, Real estate development, SCPI, etc.)
- **Team values** section highlighting their collaborative approach and client focus
- **Prodige - Experts France network** affiliation badge
- **Quick access cards** for News, Key Figures, and Spotlight sections

### 2. Our Missions (Nos Missions)
- **Services overview** with clean iconography
- **Expertise areas**: Financial, Fiscal, Social, Legal, IT, and Management consulting
- **Professional banner image** emphasizing their comprehensive approach

### 3. Information Hub (Base d'informations)
- **Key Figures section** organized by category:
  - Fiscal figures (IR, IS, ISF...)
  - Social figures (SMIC, transport allowances, social contributions...)
  - Legal figures (rates and indices)
  - Patrimonial figures (rental indices, interest rates...)
- **News/Actualités** section with latest updates
- **Zoom Sur...** spotlight section

### 4. Practical Tools (Outils Pratiques)
- **Calculators & simulators** section
- Clean layout with descriptions of each available tool
- Call-to-action to contact the firm for personalized advice

### 5. Contact Page (Nous Contacter)
- **Contact information** prominently displayed:
  - Address: 9 rue de Clichy, 75009 Paris
  - Phone: 01 42 65 77 22
  - Fax: 01 42 65 77 55
- **Functional contact form** with name, first name, email, phone, subject, and message fields (connected to backend)
- **Interactive Google Maps** showing the office location

### 6. Access Map (Plan d'accès)
- **Full-screen interactive map** centered on the office
- Contact details sidebar

### 7. English Version (Our Firm)
- Complete English translation of key content
- Same elegant design as French version

---

### Navigation & Layout
- **Modern sticky header** with the Cabinet PH logo and navigation menu
- **Language toggle** (FR/EN) in the header
- **Mobile-responsive hamburger menu**
- **Elegant footer** with contact info, quick links, and legal mentions

### Backend Integration
- **Lovable Cloud** for the contact form submission
- Emails stored in database and/or sent to the firm's email

### Animations & Polish
- Fade-in animations on scroll
- Smooth page transitions
- Hover effects on interactive elements
- Professional loading states
